"Description"

Tequila sunrise but with Corona® and lime... drink responsibly!

"Ingredients"

  • 1 (12 ounce) bottle lager beer (such as Corona®)
  • 1 fluid ounce tequila
  • 1 fluid ounce orange juice
  • 2 teaspoons grenadine syrup
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice

"Steps"

  1. Step 1

    2. Open the Corona® bottle and drink to where beer is level with the top of the label.

  2. Step 2

    3. Pour in tequila, orange juice, grenadine, and lime juice. Place thumb over top of bottle, turn upside-down and back up to combine.
