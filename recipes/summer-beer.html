"Description"

Lemonade with a kick!

"Ingredients"

  • 4 cups ice cubes
  • 1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle beer
  • 12 fluid ounces vodka

"Steps"

  1. Step 1

    2. In a blender crush the ice. Pour in the lemonade concentrate, beer and vodka. Blend until smooth. Pour into frozen glasses.
